45 Pine St

-

For the first time in over 20 years, 45 Pine Street is offered for sale. The current owners purchased a simple colonial in the heart of historic Exeter New Hampshire and transformed it into a luxury estate, comprised of a gym, theater, wine cellar, formal space, family space, a heated pool with waterfall and heated poolhouse. Outdoor living includes a bocce court surfaced with red clay, a hidden sculpture garden, many small informal gathering spaces as well as the capacity for entertaining hundreds. The fully irrigated mature landscape comprises of carefully selected species of trees inspired by the Boston Public Gardens. Recent upgrades in the facility include new heating system (2017) and a fully resurfaced pool (2018). This home is turn-key ready to move in and enjoy.
LIFESTYLE
In one of the most pristine towns in New England sits an estate of understated elegance. Originally built in the late 40's, the home was lovingly restored in 2000 and expanded to more than double its original size. In 2008, the owners acquired the ajoining rear parsel to create an estate like no other on the seacoast of NH. While interior to the town of Exeter and adjacent to the prestegous Phillip Exeter Academy, 45 Pine is designed in a way that offers unmatched privacy while also being part of the frabic of this picturesque town. Walk to parks, restaurants, shopping, museums and all that the Academy has to offer, and at the end of the day, retreat to an oasis comprised of mature plantings, water features and stone walls.
